### 🟢 PresenceIX  ·  AI Automation & Agent Systems

PresenceIX builds and deploys autonomous AI systems for med spas and high-value service businesses. We design the full stack — from the AI phone agent that answers missed calls to the automated pipeline that reactivates a client database of 2,000 people in 72 hours.

The core problem we solve: med spas and aesthetics clinics miss 30–60% of inbound calls during treatments. Each missed call is a $300–$800 appointment gone. Their leads sit unanswered for hours. Their past clients never hear from them again. PresenceIX eliminates every one of those revenue leaks — automatically.

**What we deploy:**

**AI Phone Agent** — Answers every missed call 24/7, qualifies the caller, and books appointments directly into the clinic’s scheduling system. No receptionist required after hours.

**5-Second Lead Follow-Up** — The moment a lead fills out a form, sends a DM, or clicks an ad, an AI-powered sequence responds within 5 seconds via SMS and email. 78% of leads go to the first business that responds. We make sure that’s always our client.

**Client Reactivation Campaigns** — Every med spa has 500–2,000 past clients sitting dormant in their database. We deploy targeted AI-personalized SMS campaigns that bring them back. Most clients see $5,000–$30,000 in recovered revenue within the first 72 hours.

**Automated Review Generation** — After every appointment, the system sends a perfectly timed review request via SMS. More 5-star reviews. Higher Google ranking. More inbound leads. All on autopilot.

**Performance Dashboard** — Clients get a real-time view of every lead, every response, every booking, and every dollar recovered — without logging into five different tools.

## ✦ What I Build

### AI Agent Systems

Autonomous multi-step agent pipelines designed to handle entire business workflows without human intervention. Built on n8n, Claude API, and custom orchestration layers, these agents handle research, outreach, lead qualification, appointment booking, follow-up sequences, and client communication — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

The difference between a basic automation and an AI agent system: automations follow fixed rules. Agent systems reason, adapt, and make decisions based on context. When a lead says “I’m not ready yet,” the agent doesn’t stop — it schedules a follow-up for the right time and adjusts the messaging accordingly.

### Business Automation

End-to-end workflow automation that connects every tool a business uses into one unified revenue operation. CRMs, booking platforms, communication tools, payment processors, and AI models — all talking to each other, all triggered automatically, all removing manual work from the equation.

Every system I build follows one principle: one trigger, ten downstream actions, zero manual steps. A new lead comes in — the CRM is updated, the follow-up is sent, the owner is notified, the appointment slot is held, and the onboarding sequence begins. All before a human touches anything.

### SaaS Products

The agency model validates the problem and builds the playbook. The SaaS model scales the solution to hundreds of businesses simultaneously. Currently productizing the PresenceIX automation system into a self-serve SaaS platform — AI automation packaged specifically for med spas at $299/mo per clinic. No setup required. No agency retainer. Just deploy and run.

### Web & Digital Presence

Production-grade websites and brand systems built with Next.js, Framer, and AI-accelerated tooling. Every project is built for speed, conversion, and long-term maintainability. No templates. No page builders. No 6-month timelines.
