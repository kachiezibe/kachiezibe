## About

I build **AI automation systems and autonomous agent pipelines** that turn manual, broken, and slow business operations into self-running revenue machines.

The thesis is simple: most businesses don't have a talent problem — they have a systems problem. A missed call is a lost appointment. A slow follow-up is a lost client. A manual process is a ceiling on growth. I remove those ceilings.

I run two companies. One handles **web design and digital presence** for small businesses. The other deploys **AI automation and agent systems** for high-value service businesses — systems that respond to leads in seconds, qualify prospects automatically, reactivate dormant clients, and generate reviews on autopilot.

Everything I build is designed to compound. Not just solve a problem once — but keep solving it, at scale, without adding headcount.

## Story

```
2023  Built a website for my uncle's restaurant in two weeks.
      His bookings doubled. The realization: most small businesses
      don't have a talent problem. They have a systems problem.
      That was the beginning.

2024  Went deep on automation — n8n, AI APIs, agent orchestration.
      Built a lead follow-up system for a real estate brokerage.
      They went from losing 40% of inbound leads to responding
      to every single one in under 5 seconds.

2024  Repositioned PresenceIX. Pivoted exclusively to AI automation
      and agent systems for med spas and aesthetics clinics.
      Narrow niche. Expensive problem. Undeniable ROI.

2025  Identified a second market: HVAC companies losing $150–300K
      per year to missed calls. Designed the architecture for
      a fully autonomous AI phone agent system.

2026  Launched Folio Studio as a standalone brand.
      Two companies. Two markets. One operator.
      Currently building toward $1M ARR.
```
